The "Other" Widow

A mysterious woman in black who claims to be Cornelius's *first* wife, who everyone thought was dead.

Her Secret

She's not his first wife. She's an actress hired by Julian (the son) to contest the will and create chaos.

Background

Cassandra Noir is a struggling actress from Los Angeles who specializes in dramatic roles and has a talent for adopting different personas. When Julian Crimson contacted her through a seedy talent agency, she was immediately intrigued by the role of a lifetime: playing a dead woman who has returned from the grave to claim what's rightfully hers.

Motive

Cassandra was hired by Julian to create confusion and legal complications around Cornelius's will. If she could successfully pose as his first wife, she could contest the will and potentially delay its execution for years, giving Julian time to find another solution to his debt problems. She's been paid handsomely for her performance, but the plan requires Cornelius to be dead for it to work.

Opportunity

As part of her role, Cassandra arrived at the cabin with forged documentation and a detailed backstory about her "marriage" to Cornelius. Her acting skills and research into the family's history make her convincing enough to fool most people, at least temporarily. Her partnership with Julian gives her inside knowledge of family dynamics and potential weaknesses to exploit.

Alibi

Claims she was visiting Cornelius's grave. She says she wanted to pay her respects before the storm got worse.

Suspicious Behavior

  • She seems to be performing grief rather than genuinely feeling it
  • Has been seen coordinating with Julian when they think no one is watching
  • Her "memories" of Cornelius don't quite match up with known facts
  • Carries what appear to be forged marriage documents
  • Shows theatrical mannerisms that suggest professional acting training
  • Seems more interested in legal matters than in mourning
