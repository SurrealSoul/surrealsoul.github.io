The Business Rival

A ruthless corporate raider who Cornelius nearly bankrupted. He claims he's here to "pay his respects."

His Secret

He wasn't invited. He paid the groundskeeper to let him in. He's been trying to find Cornelius's hidden ledger to ruin his company.

Background

Marco Varelli earned his nickname "The Shark" through decades of ruthless corporate takeovers and business dealings. He's known for circling wounded companies like a predator, waiting for the perfect moment to strike. Five years ago, Cornelius outmaneuvered him in a deal that cost Marco millions and his reputation on Wall Street.

Motive

Marco's business empire has been slowly crumbling since his defeat by Cornelius. He believes that Cornelius keeps a secret ledger detailing illegal business practices that could be used to destroy the Crimson corporation from the inside. With Cornelius dead, Marco hopes to find this ledger and use it to reclaim his lost fortune and reputation.

Opportunity

Though uninvited, Marco gained access to the cabin by bribing Captain Jack, the groundskeeper. He's been secretly searching the property for Cornelius's hidden safe or office where the ledger might be kept. His business background means he knows exactly what to look for and how to cover his tracks.

Alibi

Claims he was making a late-night business call. He admits there was no signal, but says he was recording a voice memo.
