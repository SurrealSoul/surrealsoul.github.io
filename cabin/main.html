Murder at the Cabin

The Setup

19 people have gathered at the isolated mountain cabin for Cornelius Crimson's annual winter dinner party - a lavish tradition he's hosted for the past decade. A raging blizzard has cut off all roads and communication lines. You are trapped.

This morning, Cornelius Crimson was found dead in his study... **an empty vial of poison clutched in his hand.** It looks like a suicide, but the door was locked from the *outside*. One of you is a killer. It's up to the rest of you to find out who before they strike again.

The Victim

Cornelius Crimson (The Host)

An eccentric and wealthy billionaire who loved hosting elaborate gatherings. He invited you all to his annual winter celebration, but someone turned his final party into their last supper.

As you investigate the scene, several disturbing clues emerge:

  • A muddy boot print was found near the fireplace - too small to belong to a man
  • A single red sequin was discovered clutched in Cornelius's hand
  • The study window was unlocked, but the snow outside remains undisturbed
  • An empty vial labeled "Dr. Reed's Medical Supplies" was found, but it contained spider venom, not her usual medications
  • The dinner table was set for exactly 18 people, but only 17 guests arrived
  • Cornelius's wine glass from last night's toast shows traces of an unknown substance
  • The grandfather clock in the study stopped at 3:17 AM

The killer is among you. Find them before the blizzard clears... or you're next.
