The Maid

A new, quiet maid who seems terrified of everyone. She was seen weeping in the hallway this morning.

Her Secret

She isn't a maid. She's the illegitimate daughter of Cornelius, here to claim her birthright. She has a locket with his picture in it.

Background

Genevieve grew up in poverty, raised by a single mother who worked as a seamstress. For 22 years, she believed her father had died before she was born. Only recently did her dying mother reveal the truth: her father was Cornelius Crimson, who had abandoned them when he learned of the pregnancy. She's spent months planning how to approach him and claim her place in the family.

Motive

Genevieve came to the cabin hoping to reveal herself as Cornelius's daughter and claim her inheritance. She took the job as a maid to get close to him and find the right moment to present evidence of their relationship. However, when she finally worked up the courage to confront him, he cruelly rejected her, claiming she was just another gold-digger trying to scam money from him.

Opportunity

As a maid, Genevieve has access to every room in the cabin and knows everyone's routines. She's been observing the family dynamics and learning about each guest's secrets while cleaning their rooms. Her invisibility as "just the help" means people often don't notice her presence or guard their conversations around her.

Alibi

Claims she was cleaning the upstairs hallway. She says she was too terrified of the storm to sleep.
