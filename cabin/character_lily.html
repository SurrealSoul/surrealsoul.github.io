The Chef's Daughter

Chef Antoine's silent, pale daughter. She moves without sound, and sometimes... without opening doors.

Her Secret

She died of a fever three years ago. Or at least, that's what Antoine's personnel file says. She doesn't breathe, and she's very cold to the touch.

Background

Lily Dubois is... present. Antoine claims she is his daughter, but he refuses to look directly at her. She has no records of entering the country, and no one recalls seeing her arrive at the cabin. She simply appeared one evening in the kitchen, pale as moonlight and silent as the grave. She wanders the cabin at all hours, often seen in two places at once.

Motive

Lily doesn't appear to have a direct motive to kill Cornelius, but her access to the master key and her tendency to wander the cabin unseen makes her a perfect witness - or accomplice. Her loyalty to her father might drive her to help cover up his crimes, or her childish sense of justice might motivate her to take action against someone she perceives as having wronged her family.

Opportunity

Lily stole the master key from Arthur's office, giving her access to every room in the cabin. Her small size and quiet nature allow her to move through the building unnoticed, and her habit of exploring means she's likely discovered secret passages and hiding spots that adults don't know about. She also has access to her father's kitchen and all its potential weapons.

Alibi

Claims she was asleep in her room. However, Genevieve claims she saw her wandering the halls alone.

Suspicious Behavior

  • She rarely speaks, and when she does, it sounds like wind in the eaves
  • Has been seen walking through closed doors
  • Leaves no footprints in the snow
  • The temperature drops 10 degrees whenever she enters a room
  • Electronic devices malfunction in her presence
  • Claims to speak with the "other guests" who aren't there
  • Appears in photographs as a blur or orb
  • Her father seems terrified of her
