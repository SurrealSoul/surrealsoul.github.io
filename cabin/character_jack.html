A grizzled, one-eyed man who lives in a shack on the property. He handles the generators and snowplows.

His Secret

He's the one who sabotaged the snowplow and cut the phone lines. He was paid by someone... but who?

Background

"Captain" Jack McReady is a former Navy man who lost his eye in combat and his way in civilian life. He's worked as groundskeeper for the Crimson cabin for the past decade, maintaining the generators, plowing the roads, and keeping the property secure. His military background makes him resourceful and capable of handling any situation, but also prone to following orders without question.

Motive

Jack doesn't appear to have a personal vendetta against Cornelius, but his dire financial situation makes him vulnerable to bribery. Someone paid him handsomely to ensure that no one could leave the cabin this weekend - cutting the phone lines and sabotaging the snowplow to create the perfect isolated crime scene. The question remains: who hired him, and did they tell him why?

Opportunity

As the groundskeeper, Jack controls all the cabin's essential systems and security. He knows every entrance and exit, has access to all the utility areas, and understands exactly how to cut the property off from the outside world. His military training also means he knows how to follow orders and keep secrets under pressure.

Alibi

Claims he was checking the generator in the shed. He says the storm was threatening to cut the power lines.
