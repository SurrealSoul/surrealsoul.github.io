The Personal Physician

The prim and proper doctor who was on call 24/7 to manage Cornelius's "condition."

Her Secret

Cornelius was blackmailing her. He knew she faked her medical license and threatened to expose her.

Background

Dr. Evelyn Reed appears to be the picture of medical professionalism - always impeccably dressed, carrying her black medical bag, and speaking in measured, clinical tones. However, beneath her composed exterior lies a web of deception that began years ago when she forged her medical credentials to escape poverty.

Motive

Cornelius discovered her secret through his extensive background checks and has been using it to control her completely. He forced her to be available 24/7, paid her below market rate, and threatened to destroy her life if she ever tried to leave. The stress of living under constant blackmail has pushed her to the breaking point.

Opportunity

As Cornelius's personal physician, Dr. Reed had unlimited access to him and intimate knowledge of his health conditions. She also had access to a wide variety of medical supplies and drugs, including exotic compounds that could easily be mistaken for natural causes of death. The poison vial found at the scene came from her medical bag.

Alibi

Claims she was reviewing medical records in her room. She says she was preparing a case study on Cornelius's condition.
