Beatrice Crimson (ID: F8)

Role: The Spiteful Sister

Key Facts

  • Secret: She has a collection of rare poisons, a hobby she and Cornelius shared. She was seen leaving the study just before the body was found.
  • Motive: Beatrice firmly believes that the Crimson fortune should have been split equally between her and her brother, or better yet, should have gone entirely to her as the elder sibling. She's spent her entire life watching Cornelius squander what she considers to be her birthright, and she's finally decided to take matters into her own hands.
  • Alibi: Claims she was reading in the library. She says she fell asleep in the armchair around 2 AM.
