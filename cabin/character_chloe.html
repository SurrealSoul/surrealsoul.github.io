The Personal Assistant

Cornelius's efficient and overworked assistant. She handled all his appointments and correspondence.

Her Secret

She was secretly siphoning money from Cornelius's accounts to pay for her mother's medical care. He found out yesterday.

Background

Chloe has been Cornelius's personal assistant for three years, managing everything from his schedule to his personal correspondence. She's known for her efficiency and discretion, but recent family troubles have pushed her to desperate measures. Her mother's battle with cancer has drained her savings and driven her to make choices she never thought she'd make.

Motive

When her mother was diagnosed with an aggressive form of cancer, Chloe faced astronomical medical bills that her insurance wouldn't cover. She began skimming small amounts from Cornelius's numerous accounts, thinking he'd never notice. But Cornelius discovered her theft yesterday and threatened to have her arrested unless she repaid every penny within a week - money she doesn't have.

Opportunity

As his personal assistant, Chloe had access to Cornelius's calendar, knew his routines, and had keys to most areas of the cabin. She also knew about his meeting with the lawyer and might have overheard details about the will. Her access to his correspondence means she could have learned about other guests' secrets as well.

Alibi

Claims she was organizing Cornelius's schedule for the next day. She says she was working late to catch up on emails.
