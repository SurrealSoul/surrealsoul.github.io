The "Local Cop"

A man in a police uniform who arrived just before the storm. He claims he was checking on a distress call.

His Secret

He's not a cop. He's a private investigator hired by Cornelius. He discovered that "Eleanor" is actually a wanted fraudster named Lorelei Vance, and he was planning to turn her in... for a price.

Background

Frank Dunn is a former police officer who lost his job due to allegations of corruption and evidence tampering. Since then, he's worked as a private investigator, specializing in surveillance and information gathering for wealthy clients who need to know what their "friends" and family are really up to. His police uniform is a prop that helps him gain access to places where he wouldn't normally be welcome.

Motive

Cornelius hired Dunn weeks ago to investigate several of his guests, suspecting that some of them were planning something against him. Dunn was supposed to gather evidence and report back, but when he learned about Cornelius's plans to cut certain people out of his will, he realized that his client's death might be more profitable than his continued employment.

Opportunity

Dunn's false police credentials gave him immediate access to the cabin and authority that no one questioned. His investigative skills and surveillance equipment allowed him to gather detailed information about everyone's secrets and motivations. As a former cop, he also knows how to stage a crime scene and misdirect an investigation.

Alibi

Claims he was patrolling the perimeter. He says he was checking for any signs of forced entry.

Suspicious Behavior

  • His police credentials don't check out with the local department
  • Shows an unusual knowledge of each guest's background and secrets
  • Has been taking notes and photos throughout his stay
  • Seems more interested in observing than investigating the murder
  • Carries surveillance equipment not typically issued to local police
  • His radio doesn't seem to connect to any actual police dispatch
  • Was overheard making calls about "completing the contract"
  • Was seen handing a manila folder to Eleanor and whispering "I know everything"
