Madam Zora (ID: M8)

Role: The Fortune Teller

Key Facts

  • Secret: She's a complete fraud. She's also Beatrice's (the sister's) paid accomplice, hired to "spook" Cornelius into changing his will in Beatrice's favor.
  • Motive: Beatrice Crimson hired Madam Zora to psychologically manipulate Cornelius into changing his will in her favor. The plan was for Zora to fill Cornelius with dread about his mortality and guilt about not taking care of his "dear sister." However, when Cornelius saw through the charade and threatened to expose both women, Zora realized she needed to take more drastic action to protect herself.
  • Alibi: Claims she was meditating in the conservatory. She says the spirits were particularly restless that night.
