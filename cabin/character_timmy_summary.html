Timmy Crimson (ID: A3)

Role: The "Forgotten" Nephew

Key Facts

  • Secret: He's not clumsy, he's a professional cat burglar. He was seen practicing his lock-picking skills on the kitchen pantry.
  • Motive: Timmy has been systematically stealing from the Crimson family for years, taking small items that wouldn't be missed immediately. However, Cornelius recently discovered his activities and threatened to turn him in to the police unless he returned everything he'd stolen. Facing prison time and the loss of his lucrative criminal career, Timmy decided he needed to permanently silence his uncle.
  • Alibi: Claims he was playing video games in his room. He says he had his noise-canceling headphones on.
