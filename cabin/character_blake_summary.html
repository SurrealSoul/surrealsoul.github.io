Blake Riley (ID: B2)

Role: The Bodyguard

Key Facts

  • Secret: He is Eleanor's secret lover. He would do anything to protect her and ensure their future together.
  • Motive: Blake is deeply in love with Eleanor and knows about the prenuptial agreement that leaves her penniless unless Cornelius dies under unnatural circumstances. He's also been promised a substantial sum from Eleanor's inheritance once she receives it. His military training makes him capable of carrying out a perfect murder, and his devotion to Eleanor makes him willing to do anything to secure their future together.
  • Alibi: Claims he was doing security rounds outside. He says he saw footprints in the snow but thought they were from an animal.
