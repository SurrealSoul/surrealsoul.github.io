Julian Crimson (ID: A5)

Role: The Disowned Son

Key Facts

  • Secret: He is dangerously in debt to a loan shark and was heard screaming, "I'll get what's mine, one way or another!"
  • Motive: Julian's debts to dangerous people have reached a critical point. He owes over $2 million to a loan shark who has threatened his life. With his father's death, he hopes to inherit enough to pay off his debts and reclaim his place in high society. His father's will was his last hope for survival.
  • Alibi: Claims he was smoking a cigarette on the back porch. He says the storm was too loud to hear anything inside.
