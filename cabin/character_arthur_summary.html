Arthur Doyle (ID: G3)

Role: The Butler

Key Facts

  • Secret: Cornelius was planning to fire him without a pension after 40 years of service. He also knows the location of the cabin's secret passageways.
  • Motive: After 40 years of faithful service, Arthur discovered that Cornelius planned to dismiss him without a pension, claiming budget cuts. The revelation that his lifelong dedication meant nothing to his employer has filled him with a rage that simmers beneath his professional exterior. At 65, he has no other prospects and no savings.
  • Alibi: Claims he was polishing silver in the pantry. He says he finds repetitive tasks soothing when he can't sleep.
