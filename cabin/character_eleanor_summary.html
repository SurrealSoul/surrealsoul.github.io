Eleanor Crimson (ID: Q2)

Role: The Widow

Key Facts

  • Secret: She is actually Lorelei Vance, a wanted con artist from Chicago. Officer Dunn may be hot on your case.
  • Motive: The pre-nuptial agreement she signed was ironclad - she would receive nothing if they divorced or if Cornelius died of natural causes. However, if he were to die under "unnatural circumstances," she would inherit a substantial portion of his estate. Her secret relationship with Blake Riley, her bodyguard, gives her additional motivation to secure their future together.
  • Alibi: Claims she was sleeping in the master bedroom. She says she took a sleeping pill at 10 PM and didn't wake up until the scream.
