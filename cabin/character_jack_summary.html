"Captain" Jack (ID: Q9)

Role: The Groundskeeper

Key Facts

  • Secret: He's the one who sabotaged the snowplow and cut the phone lines. He was paid by someone... but who?
  • Motive: Jack doesn't appear to have a personal vendetta against Cornelius, but his dire financial situation makes him vulnerable to bribery. Someone paid him handsomely to ensure that no one could leave the cabin this weekend - cutting the phone lines and sabotaging the snowplow to create the perfect isolated crime scene. The question remains: who hired him, and did they tell him why?
  • Alibi: Claims he was checking the generator in the shed. He says the storm was threatening to cut the power lines.
