Chef Antoine (ID: W6)

Role: The Private Chef

Key Facts

  • Secret: Cornelius gave him a bad review that ruined his last restaurant. He has access to all the food... and a very sharp set of knives.
  • Motive: Two years ago, Cornelius dined at Antoine's restaurant and was apparently dissatisfied with his meal. His scathing review, published in a major culinary magazine, was so influential that it led to the restaurant's closure within months. Antoine lost his life savings, his reputation, and his dream. He's been forced to take private catering jobs to survive, and this weekend's event was supposed to be his comeback opportunity.
  • Alibi: Claims he was prepping dough for the morning croissants. He says the kitchen is the only warm place in this drafty cabin.
