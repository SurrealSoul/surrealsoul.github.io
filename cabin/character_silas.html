The Family Lawyer

The man who is here to read the will. He seems nervous and is carrying a locked briefcase.

His Secret

He's the only one who knows what's in the will. The will contains a new clause, added yesterday, that makes him the primary beneficiary.

Background

Silas Croft has been the Crimson family attorney for over 20 years, handling everything from business contracts to personal legal matters. He's always been seen as trustworthy and ethical, but recent financial troubles have tested his moral boundaries. He's been with Cornelius through thick and thin, which apparently earned him more than just legal fees.

Motive

Yesterday, just before the gathering, Cornelius called Silas with an urgent request to add a codicil to his will. In a moment of what Cornelius called "gratitude for faithful service," he made Silas the primary beneficiary, cutting out most of his family. Silas now stands to inherit the bulk of the Crimson fortune - but only if Cornelius dies before he can change his mind again.

Opportunity

As the family lawyer, Silas had private meetings with Cornelius and access to his study. He arrived at the cabin early to "prepare for the reading" and was seen having intense conversations with Cornelius throughout the evening. His legal knowledge would also help him understand exactly how to ensure the will couldn't be contested.

Alibi

Claims he was preparing documents in the guest study. He says he needed to have the will ready for the morning reading.

Suspicious Behavior

  • Seems unusually nervous for someone just doing their job
  • Has been evasive when asked about the contents of the will
  • Was overheard making a phone call about "securing his future"
  • Insists on reading the will immediately, despite the tragedy
  • Has been asking odd questions about the timeline of Cornelius's death
