Lily (ID: Y9)

Role: The Chef's Daughter

Key Facts

  • Secret: She stole the master key from the butler's office. She's been all over the cabin and... she says she saw the killer.
  • Motive: Lily doesn't appear to have a direct motive to kill Cornelius, but her access to the master key and her tendency to wander the cabin unseen makes her a perfect witness - or accomplice. Her loyalty to her father might drive her to help cover up his crimes, or her childish sense of justice might motivate her to take action against someone she perceives as having wronged her family.
  • Alibi: Claims she was asleep in her room. However, Genevieve claims she saw her wandering the halls alone.
