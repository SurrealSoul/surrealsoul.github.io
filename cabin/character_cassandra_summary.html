Cassandra (ID: B6)

Role: The "Other" Widow

Key Facts

  • Secret: She's not his first wife. She's an actress hired by Julian (the son) to contest the will and create chaos.
  • Motive: Cassandra was hired by Julian to create confusion and legal complications around Cornelius's will. If she could successfully pose as his first wife, she could contest the will and potentially delay its execution for years, giving Julian time to find another solution to his debt problems. She's been paid handsomely for her performance, but the plan requires Cornelius to be dead for it to work.
  • Alibi: Claims she was visiting Cornelius's grave. She says she wanted to pay her respects before the storm got worse.
